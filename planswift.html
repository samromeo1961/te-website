Measure Smarter, Not Harder

Complete your takeoffs in a fraction of the time

PDF Import

Import PDF plans directly into PlanSwift. Support for multi-page documents, high-resolution images, and automatic scale detection.

Area Takeoff

Click to trace around rooms, floors, or any area. PlanSwift calculates square metres instantly with automatic deductions for openings.

Linear Takeoff

Measure walls, pipes, cables, and any linear element. Click along the path and get total linear metres with segment breakdown.

Count Takeoff

Click on items to count them - doors, windows, fixtures, fittings. Automatic totalling with visual markers on the plan.

Excel Export

Export your quantities directly to Excel spreadsheets. Format exactly how you need for pricing, reporting, or importing elsewhere.

Assemblies

Build assemblies that calculate multiple items from a single measurement. Measure a wall once and get studs, plaster, paint, and more.

Simple 3-Step Workflow

From plans to quantities in minutes

1

Import Plans

Drag and drop your PDF plans into PlanSwift. Set the scale using a known dimension on the drawing.

2

Measure

Select your measurement type and click on the plan. Areas, lengths, and counts are calculated automatically.

3

Export

Send your quantities to Excel, or directly into your estimating software via integration.
