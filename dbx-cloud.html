Real-Time Dashboard

Get a complete overview of your business with live KPIs, job status tracking, and actionable insights. Monitor project progress, track financials, and stay on top of deadlines.

  • Live KPI cards and metrics
  • Job progress tracking
  • Recent activity feed
  • Quick navigation to all modules

02

Bill of Quantities Editor

A powerful, spreadsheet-like interface powered by AG Grid. Edit quantities, descriptions, and prices with formula support. Organize by cost centres and generate reports instantly.

  • Formula support: 10*2.5, sqrt(144)
  • Cost centre tree navigation
  • Inline editing with validation
  • Add items from catalogue
  • Supplier assignment
03

Accounting & Payments

Complete financial management including invoice entry, payment batches, progress claims, and budget tracking. Match invoices to orders and generate ABA files for batch payments.

  • Invoice entry and matching
  • Payment batch generation
  • ABA file export for banks
  • Progress claims management
  • Budget vs actual tracking
04

Document Management

Centralized document storage with version control, drawing registers, and transmittal tracking. Upload drawings, contracts, and specifications with full revision history.

  • Drawing register with revisions
  • Transmittal creation and tracking
  • PDF viewer and markup
  • Version history
  • Document attributes
