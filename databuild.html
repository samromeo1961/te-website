Comprehensive Estimating & Job Management

Everything you need to run your construction business

Bill of Quantities

Create detailed BOQs with cost centres, multiple price levels, and automatic calculations. Import from catalogues or build from scratch.

Job Costing

Track actual costs against estimates in real-time. Monitor job profitability and identify variances before they become problems.

Purchase Orders

Generate purchase orders directly from your BOQ. Track deliveries, manage suppliers, and control project spending.

Progress Claims

Manage client claims with percentage complete tracking. Generate professional claim documents with full backup details.

Item Catalogue

Build and maintain a comprehensive catalogue of materials and labour items with multiple price levels and supplier links.

Reports

Generate detailed reports for quotes, variations, cost tracking, and management summaries. Customisable to your needs.
