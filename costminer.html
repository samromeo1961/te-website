Professional Cost Management Tools

Everything quantity surveyors need in one platform

Cost Planning

Create early-stage cost plans using elemental or trade-based structures. Build benchmarks from historical data and adjust for project specifics.

Detailed Estimating

Build comprehensive estimates with full measurement backup. Support for multiple measurement methods and automatic calculations.

Rate Libraries

Build and maintain comprehensive rate libraries. Share rates across your organisation and keep pricing current with market updates.

Cost Analysis

Analyse costs by element, trade, or custom breakdown. Compare estimates, track revisions, and benchmark against historical projects.

Team Collaboration

Work together in real-time with your team. Role-based access controls ensure the right people see the right information.

Cloud Access

Access your projects from anywhere. No software to install, automatic updates, and secure cloud storage for all your data.
