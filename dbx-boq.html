Powerful Features for Construction Professionals

Everything you need to manage your Bill of Quantities efficiently

01

Bill of Quantities Editor

A modern, spreadsheet-like interface powered by AG Grid. Edit quantities, descriptions, and prices with ease. Enter mathematical formulas directly in quantity fields - no more switching to a calculator.

  • Formula support: 10*2.5, sqrt(144), PI*r^2
  • Recipe explosion into sub-items
  • Cost centre navigation
  • Variable substitution
  • Inline editing with validation
02

Claims & Contract Management

Complete toolkit for managing progress claims, cost-plus billing, and contract variations. Track every dollar with comprehensive audit trails.

  • Progress claims with retention
  • Cost-plus contract billing
  • Contract variation orders
  • Payment receipt tracking
  • Complete audit history
03

Purchase Order Generation

Generate professional purchase orders directly from your BOQ items. Manage suppliers, track orders, and maintain a complete procurement history.

  • One-click PO generation
  • Supplier management with ABN lookup
  • Customizable PO templates
  • Order status tracking
  • Email integration
04

Catalogue & Recipe Management

Access and manage your entire Databuild price catalogue. Create and edit recipes, manage supplier pricing, and keep your rates up to date.

  • Full catalogue browser
  • Recipe ingredient management
  • Multiple price levels
  • Supplier price tracking
  • Search and filter
05

Job Management Dashboard

Comprehensive job tracking with detailed project information, contract values, and real-time progress monitoring. Keep all your project data organized in one place.

  • Job details and contract info
  • Progress tracking overview
  • Client and contact management
  • Quick access to BOQ, orders, claims
  • Multi-job navigation

Built with modern technologies for reliability and performance

  • Chrome: Version 90 or later
  • Firefox: Version 88 or later

  • Safari: Version 14 or later
